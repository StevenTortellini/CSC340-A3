About us


DoggyDaycare was founded by Steven Torres in the fall of 2020.
He was taking care of the family dog while the rest of the family was out of town.
His friends took notice and one by one they eached asked if he could watch their dogs.
By the end of the day he was left with 8 dogs he had to look after. In disbelief he exclaimed "It looks like a DoggyDaycare in here" and thats when the idea was born.
Our first location opened summer of 2021 and it been a good time since. We are proud to serve the triad area with the best quality care for your dogs.
